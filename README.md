# Soda Smash

![Soda Smash - Title](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/banyapon/banyapon.github.io/refs/heads/main/images/sodasmash1.png)

## Gameplay & Features

* **The Ultimate Party Game**: While you can play solo, the game truly shines with a Joystick/Controller.
* **Versatile Game Modes**: Includes Single Player, Dual Mode, Assistant with AI, and Player VS Player.
* **Local Multiplayer**: Supports up to 4-player Local Co-op/Versus—grab your friends and hit the court! *(We added this restriction to "Summer Party!" because we like it and want people to play it together.)*
* **8 Unique Competitors**: Choose from a diverse roster of 8 playable characters, each featuring unique stats and special skills. Whether you prefer raw power, lightning speed, or precision defense, there's a character to match your playstyle.
* **Retro Aesthetic**: Designed under specific "RIGHT ON TIME", "OVERHEAT", and "SWEATY PALMS" constraints to maintain a stylistically consistent, low-fidelity 3D charm.
